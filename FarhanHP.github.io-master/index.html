What is GuildHub ?

GuildHub is a guild system for World of Warcraft (WoW) players. GuildHub's goal is to simplify WoW players that want to do some simple things within the game such as seeing basic informations about Your WoW account, exiting and joining guild, and manage the jobs, all are done without have to log in to the game. GuildHub also has guild forum so that You and other guild members can use it to discuss everything about Your guild. In order to do that, You just simply access GuildHub website by using an internet connected device. If You don't have an account, You can use your favorite platform such as Google, Github, Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn to register.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I join GuildHub ?

Just visit this page and fill informations needed then click a register button. You also can use Gmail to register so that the form is filled with informations suit your Gmail.

Q2: Who can post a job ?

Every GuildHub member who has joined a guild can post a job.

Q3: Are there any criterias of acceptable posted jobs ?

No, you can post jobs as you please, but you need to consider that more difficult posted jobs are less likely to be taken by adventurers. Don't worry, you can overcome this situation by granting interesting rewards.

Q4: Are there any minimun requirements for granting rewards when posting a job ?

No, there are no such requirements. Even you can post a job without a reward at all, but you will recieve a consequence of less appealing posted jobs.

Q5: Are there any benefits for early members ?

Yes, you will recieve free 1,000 Gold for becoming early members.

Q6: Can you explain how a new job can be posted until the rewards are given ?

Firstly, a guild member has to post a job by filling a Posting Job form which requires him/her to fill kind of tasks, minimum rank requirement, rewards granted, and deadline of the job. Secondly, other guild members that have interest and fulfill minimum rank requrement will take the job. Lastly, a guild member that has taken the job and has finished is first will recieve the rewards.

Q7: Can you explain about membership ranks and their criteria ?

There are five membership ranks in GuildHub that are Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum. There is one thing that determines your rank, that is Point. In order to achieve a certain rank you need to increase your Point by finishing jobs. So far, you need to have 0 Point for Iron rank, 5,000 Points for Bronze rank, 15,000 Points for Silver rank, 50,000 Points for Gold rank, and 100,000 for Platinum Rank.
