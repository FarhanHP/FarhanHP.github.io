What is GuildHub ?

GuildHub is a guild system for World of Warcraft (WoW) players. By using GuildHub, You don't need to log in to the game in order to do some simple things such as seeing basic informations about Your WoW account, exiting and joining guild, and manage the jobs. GuildHub also has guild forum so that You and other guild members can use it to discuss everything about Your guild. In order to do that, You just simply access GuildHub website by using an internet connected device. If You don't have an account, You can use your favorite platform such as Google, Github, Twitter and Facebook to register.
